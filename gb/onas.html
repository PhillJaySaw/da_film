About us

Who are we?

Your reliable partner and specialist in the matter of plastic film.

We are a manufacturer of PE film products such as:

  • Protective films, construction films and industrial films
  • Waste bags, asbestos waste bags and shrink hoods,
  • Protective sheets - loose and pallet spacers on rolls.

We sell and deliver our products to customers in Western and Eastern Europe.

To meet the expectations of our clients, we send merchandise up from just one pallet of mixed items. It's the customer, who decides on what kind of products should be placed on the pallet, while maintaining the minimum quantity for a given item.

We are dealing with distribution and sale of a wide range of foil-products in whole european union.   Our customer's needs, wishes and satisfaction are most important for us, that is why we are taking care of every detail by every order. Our products are chracterised by a very high quality and their price is attractive.
