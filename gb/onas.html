About us

Who are we?

We are a manufacturer of finished PE film products such as:

  • Protective films, construction films and industrial films
  • Waste bags, asbestos waste bags and shrink hoods,
  • Protective sheets - loose and pallet spacers on rolls.

We sell and deliver our products to customers in Western and Eastern Europe.

To meet the expectations of our clients, we are sending merchandise up from just one pallet of mixed items It's the customer, who decides on what kind of products should get on the pallet, while maintaining the minimum order quantity for a given item.

The most important thing to us are the needs, wishes and satisfaction of our customers, which is why we care about every detail in the processes of order and delivery. We are proud to provide high quality goods at competitive prices.
